Yun Gu received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Biomedical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. He is currently an Assistant Professor with the Institute of Medical Robotics and the Institute of Image Processing and Pattern Recognition, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. His major research interests are computer-assisted intervention and medical image analysis.

Education
======
* Ph.D in Biomedical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 2015-2019 
* Visiting Student, Imperial College London, 2016-2018
* M.Eng. Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 2013-2015
* B.Eng. Xi'an Jiao Tong University, 2009-2013

Work experience
======
* Associate Professor, Institute of Medical Robotics, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 2023-Now
* Assistant Professor, Institute of Medical Robotics, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 2020-2023
