Yun Gu received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Biomedical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. He is currently an Assistant Professor with the Institute of Medical Robotics and the Institute of Image Processing and Pattern Recognition, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. His major research interests are computer-assisted intervention and medical image analysis.
